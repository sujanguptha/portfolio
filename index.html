    2. Short Description :

    Supply chains are evolving into automated and highly complex networks and are becoming an important source of potential benefits in the modern world. At the same time, consumers are now more interested in food product quality. However, it is challenging to track the provenance of data and maintain its traceability throughout the supply chain network. The traditional supply chains are centralized and they depend on a third party for trading. Blockchain for agriculture supply chain can empower all market players by building relationships of trust. It is a promising decentralized information technology that could benefit food supply chains by reducing transaction cost and time, increasing process transparency and security as well. The blockchain for supply chain can transform the agricultural industry by simplifying all stages of the agricultural supply chain, tracking a product along its entire path from farmland to store shelf. Furthermore, it serves as a digital solution for reducing operational costs and improving efficiency in food supply chains.
